Get to Know Us

We’re committed to championing emerging authors and the fresh work they bring. We value the exhaustive effort it takes to write a novel and love introducing compelling new voices to a larger audience. Once a year, members vote to decide which book will go down in history as the absolute best. Every winner is the kind of book you’d save from a house fire. The ones you just can’t miss. Did we mention we've been doing this for quite some time? Since our founding in 1926, we've chosen many unknown books that later became iconic works of American fiction.
